Serenity by the Sea

Sterling Puri is located in an estuary, where River Dhaudia meets the Bay of Bengal. With its own private beach, Sterling Puri is easily the best choice in this city. If you are a morning person, then don’t miss the sunrise on the sea. The renowned Puri Jagannath Temple is not far away from the resort.

Puri is known for its art form called Pattachitra, an intricate painting done on silk and cotton fabric. Fringed by the shimmering blue sea and soft golden sand, this colourful temple town looks like a large Pattachitra.

Sterling Puri has 97 well-appointed rooms, and the beach is just a hop away from your room. The lullaby of the waves will soothe your senses. Spend the day visiting the holy temple, and going on a trail to watch artists working on Pattachitra. You can take a short excursion to the renowned Konark Sun temple and Chilika lake. Don't forget to catch the sand art at the resort. For the adventurer in you, The resort's Holiday Activity Centre offers a variety of experiences like aqua zorbing, paddle boats and so on.

Dining

Utkala, the multi-cuisine restaurant is a good reason to stay at Sterling Puri. The Chefs of Odisha are known for their extraordinary culinary skills. The restaurant offers buffets and an a la carte menu to choose from. Do remember to try the Daab Chingri - tender green coconut prawn curry, and Chena Tarkari - homemade paneer kofta, during your stay.
